How OAuth Works

OAuth simplifies access to your favorite apps while keeping your data secure. Here's a quick overview of how it works:

Authorization

You grant permission to an app to access your data without sharing your password. This step ensures you maintain control over your information.

01

Access Token

The app receives an access token, a digital key that allows it to interact with your data. This token has specific permissions and expiration times for enhanced security.

02

Secure Data Sharing

The app can now access specific data you've authorized, enhancing your experience without compromising security. You remain in full control of your data permissions.
